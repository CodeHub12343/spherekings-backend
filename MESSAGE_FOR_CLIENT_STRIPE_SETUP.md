# Message to Send Your Client

Copy and send this message to your client:

---

## 📧 Email/Message Template

**Subject: Stripe Setup - Information Needed for Payment Processing**

---

Hi [Client Name],

I'm setting up Stripe payment processing for your SphereKings marketplace. To get this done, I'll need some information from you.

The good news: **You won't need to do any technical setup** - I'll handle all the configuration. You just need to provide some basic business information.

### What I Need From You:

**Personal & Business Information:**
- Your full legal name
- Official business name
- Type of business (sole owner, LLC, corporation, etc.)
- Business address
- Business phone number
- Your email address (this will be your Stripe login)

**Banking Information:**
- Bank name
- Your bank account number
- Bank routing number (for US accounts)
- Account type (Checking or Savings)
- Exact name on the bank account

**Business Details:**
- Your website URL (the marketplace domain we're building)
- Brief description of what your business does
- Your industry type
- Approximate monthly revenue (just an estimate)

**Verification Documents:**
- Photo ID (passport or driver's license - digital copy is fine)
- Proof of address (recent utility bill or bank statement)
- Business license (if you have one)

### Timeline:

1. You provide me with the above information
2. You'll create a Stripe account (I'll guide you through it)
3. You'll get your Stripe keys from your account
4. You share those keys with me securely
5. I integrate them into the application
6. **Your marketplace starts accepting live payments!**

### Security Note:

- All your banking information will only be seen by Stripe (the payment processor)
- I'll never store your bank details in the code
- I only need the keys, not your passwords

**Can you gather this information and send it to me? Just reply with the details above, and then I'll walk you through the Stripe account creation process.**

Thanks!

---

## Alternative (More Casual) Version:

Time to set up Stripe so we can start processing real payments on your marketplace! 

I need a few details from you:

**About You & Your Business:**
- Your name (for legal stuff)
- Business name
- Business address & phone
- Email (you'll use this to log into Stripe)

**Your Bank Account** (where payments will go):
- Bank name, account number, routing number
- Name on the account (must match the bank)

**Business Stuff:**
- Your website URL
- What your business does (brief description)
- What industry (apparel, entertainment, etc.)
- Rough guess on monthly revenue

**ID Verification:**
- Photo of your ID (passport/driver's license)
- Recent bill showing your address (utility bill, bank statement)

Once you send me this, you'll create a Stripe account, grab your API keys, send them to me, and we're done! I'll handle the technical part, you just handle the business info.

Let me know when you have these!
